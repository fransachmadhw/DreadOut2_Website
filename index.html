About This Game

A unique take on third-person action-adventure survival horror with Indonesian supernatural in veins. Mixed gameplay of city exploring, “DreadOut” style smartphone ghost hunting and new action-packed combat within DreadOut supernatural realm. Even though it is a Horror game in veins, but with the touch of silly high school problems, satiric social commentaries, and yet touchy memorable moments, DreadOut 2 will become a fresh take on the survival horror genre.

Main Character

Linda Melinda is the protagonist and playable character of the game. The story follows her life and struggles after the tragedy of the first game.

Read more on DreadOut Fandom >>

Ghostpedia

Ghosts are separated into two categories. Intangible and physical. Intangible Ghosts can only be damaged by taking a picture of them with the IrisPhone and Physical Ghosts can only be damaged physically with a weapon or projectile. Both types of ghosts can be stunned with Flash Blitz on the IrisPhone after Linda learns it.
